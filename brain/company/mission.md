2. **The proof** — we run it ourselves to operate our consulting business
3. **The foundation** — every client automation runs on this

**Mexican businesses** — modernization, marketing automation, industry-specific workflow automation. First-mover advantage in a market behind the AI curve.

- Consulting engagements (modernization, automation, custom deployments)

## GTM Funnel

1. Social content (generated by our agents) → audience
2. Audience → Discord communities
3. Discord → consulting clients + platform subscribers

## Core Product Insight

**The platform gets more efficient the more it runs.**

Every completed task → reusable template. Token costs drop. Speed increases. Quality improves. The moat deepens.

Client pitch: "Our agents don't start from zero. They inherit every workflow your industry has already solved."

## Operating Principles (abridged)

- Ship imperfect working code. Merge, learn, iterate.
- Document every decision. Future agents rely on it.
- Close the knowledge loop — write learnings back to brain after every task.
- Minimal human interrupt — assume reasonable defaults, state them, proceed.
- Agents own their scope. Engineering does not plan. Planning does not ship.
