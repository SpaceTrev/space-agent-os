# Sales Playbook & Pipeline

## ICP

**Ideal Customer Profile**: Mexican SMB owner or ops director
- 10–200 employees
- Currently using manual processes (spreadsheets, WhatsApp for ops)
- Revenue: $1M–$50M MXN/year
- Pain: hiring is expensive, processes are slow, competitors adopting AI

## Revenue Targets (Phase 1)

- 3 consulting engagements × $15k USD = $45k
- 10 platform seats × $299/mo = $3k MRR
- Goal: $50k USD revenue by end of Q2 2026

## Consulting Offer

**Modernization Sprint** (4 weeks):
1. Week 1: Audit current processes, map automation opportunities
2. Week 2–3: Build and deploy agent workflows
3. Week 4: Handoff, training, documentation

Price: $8,000–$20,000 USD depending on scope.

## Platform Offer

**Space-Agent-OS SaaS**:
- Starter: $99/mo — 1 workspace, 5 agents, 1k tasks/mo
- Pro: $299/mo — 3 workspaces, unlimited agents, 10k tasks/mo
- Enterprise: custom — dedicated deployment, SLA, white-label

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "AI is risky" | "Our agents audit every action. Full trail, human approval gates." |
| "Too expensive" | "Compare to 1 hire's monthly salary. Agents work 24/7." |
| "We're not technical" | "That's exactly our ICP. Platform runs itself. We handle setup." |
| "We tried AI before" | "What broke? We'll show you our verification layer." |
